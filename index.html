I'm Research Officer at Faculty of Geoinformatics, Burapha University, I research about Geo-spatial Modelling (Hydrological Model, Potential Surface Analysis, Space-Time Analysis), Remote Sensing (Optical & SAR data applied to Earthquake, Crop Monitoring, Coastal Monitoring and Coral Reef), Data science especially Machine Learning, Deep Larning & Natural Language Processing). I received Geoinformatics Master Degree and Geography Bachelor Degree from Faculty of Geoinformatics, Burapha University. and now i'm PhD (Geoinformatics) student at Srinakharinwirot University.

His primary research interests include Geospatial Technology, Data Science,Deep Learning, Natural Language Processing
